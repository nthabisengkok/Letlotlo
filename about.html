Our Aims

At Letlotlo Day Care Centre, our aim is to provide a safe, nurturing, and developmentally appropriate environment for children aged 1 to 6 years.

We focus on early childhood development through bilingual learning (English and Setswana/Sepedi), structured play, emotional support, healthy meals, and outdoor exploration.

Our goal is to help children grow socially, physically, intellectually, and emotionally while ensuring each child receives individual attention due to our small teacher-to-child ratio.

We offer:

  • Full-day and half-day care
  • Structured preschool activities
  • Healthy meals and snacks
  • Aftercare services for school-age children
  • Night shift care for working parents on late shifts or in emergencies
